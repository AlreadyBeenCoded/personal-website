## Method

1. Bring the dashi, soy sauce, mirin and sugar to a gentle simmer in a
   small pan. Add the onion and cook until translucent, about 3 minutes.
2. Add the chicken and simmer until just cooked through, 5–6 minutes.
3. Pour over two-thirds of the egg in a spiral, cover, and cook for
   about a minute — until barely set.
4. Add the rest of the egg, cover, and cut the heat. Thirty seconds:
   the top should still tremble.
5. Slide over hot rice, and finish with mitsuba.
